About My Family


Every member of the family emotionally attaches to each other in their happiness and sadness. They help each other in their bad times which give the feeling of security.

A family provides love, warmth and security to its all members throughout the life which makes it a complete family. A family can be two or more people who share goals and values, have long-term commitments to one another, and usually live in the same house. Families range from all sorts of races, forms and sizes. Families differ in various ways but each one has its own definition of a happy home and family. So, what makes a happy family? There are many important factors that make a happy family.
